Intro

This is Montserrat. The brand typeface.

Montserrat is the primary brand typeface for Instinthub. An elegant serif infused with a quirky warmth, this headline face projects trustworthiness, optimism, and sincerity.

The light weight sets an editorial tone while uncommon rounded corners infuse the typeface with a distinctive personality.

Nunito is our supporting font

Nunito is our workhouse typeface, carefully chosen to support Means in more functional moments. Purposefully plain, this sans-serif prioritizes legibility and balances the expressiveness of Means.

Helper classes

  • small-meta for 14px text
  • textcolor--secondary for light gray text (65% opacity)
  • font-weight--bold for bold text (500 weight)
  • font-weight--light for light text (300 weight)
  • font-style--italic for italics text

Less variables

  • @font-family-- Montserrat, Nunito
  • @large-copy for 17px text
  • @medium-copy for 16px text
  • @small-copy for 14px text
